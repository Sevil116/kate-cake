About me

My name is Kate, I am a mother on maternity leave and a pastry chef. I have always loved to cook, at first I only pleased my loved ones with my cakes. Thanks to the support of family and friends, I began to cook cakes to order.

For the desserts, I use only high quality natural ingredients, no flavor enhancers, egg powder, soy cream, palm oil: only natural cream, only high quality chocolate, berries are always fresh, toppings are also made specifically for a particular dessert.
